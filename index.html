I am a PhD student in the School of Computing and Data Science at The University of Hong Kong, advised by Prof. Chuan Wu. My research focuses on distributed machine learning systems, with a particular interest in optimizing training efficiency, scalability, and performance for large-scale machine learning workloads.

Prior to joining HKU, I received my Bachelor’s degree from the Department of Electronic Engineering at Tsinghua University.
